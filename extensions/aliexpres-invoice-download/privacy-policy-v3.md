### Privacy Policy for AliExpress Invoice Download extension

*Effective Date: July 5, 2026*
*Version: 3.0*

#### **Introduction**

This Privacy Policy describes how the AliExpress Invoice Download Extension ("we," "our," or "the Extension") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a business-grade invoice management experience for AliExpress users.

The Extension is **local-first**: your orders, invoices, generated PDFs, and company profiles stay on your device. The only time any personal data leaves your device is if you choose to create an optional paid account, as described below.

#### **Information We Process**

**On your device only (never transmitted to us):**
- **Order IDs & Invoice IDs** — scanned from your visible AliExpress order pages and fetched from AliExpress servers.
- **Invoice & Order PDFs** — official AliExpress documents, downloaded and cached locally for instant access, offline use, and ZIP export.
- **Generated invoices** — documents you build with the invoice generator, from your own order data.
- **Company & buyer profiles** — addresses, tax IDs, logos, and branding you save for generating invoices.
- **Settings & cache** — grouping/filter preferences, order dates, and a local free-tier usage counter.

**Transmitted only if you use the optional paid account:**
- **Email address** — used to send you a one-time sign-in code and to identify your subscription. Basis: performance of a contract (your subscription).
- **Authentication data** — the one-time code and resulting session token, used to sign you in.
- **AliExpress member identifier** — an opaque ID used to enforce the free monthly quota and prevent abuse. Basis: our legitimate interest in preventing fraud and abuse.

We do **not** collect your data for marketing, analytics, or profiling.

#### **How We Collect**

- **Page scanning** — when you open the Extension or click "Reload list," it scans the visible AliExpress orders page for order IDs and dates.
- **API requests to AliExpress** — the Extension fetches invoice IDs and PDFs from AliExpress servers using only the order IDs found on your page.
- **Filtering / search** — performed entirely on your device; nothing is sent anywhere.
- **Account actions (optional)** — only when you request a sign-in code or manage a paid plan, your email and session data are sent to our backend.

#### **Use of Information**

Your data is used only to:
1. Find, display, and download invoice PDFs for your AliExpress orders.
2. Enable bulk ZIP download, auto-prefetch, filtering, and grouping.
3. Generate branded, EU-compliant invoice PDFs from your order data.
4. Cache processed orders and PDFs for fast, offline, and bulk access.
5. Authenticate your optional paid account and enforce plan limits.

#### **Payments**

Payments for paid plans are handled entirely by **Stripe** on Stripe-hosted pages. The Extension never receives, processes, or stores your card number or transaction details. See Stripe's privacy policy at https://stripe.com/privacy.

#### **Third-Party Services**

The Extension communicates only with:
- **AliExpress** (`*.aliexpress.com`, `trade.aliexpress.com`) — to fetch invoice IDs and official PDFs.
- **Frankfurter** (`api.frankfurter.app`) — public exchange-rate data for multi-currency totals. No personal data is sent.
- **Stripe** (`checkout.stripe.com`) — hosted checkout for paid plans.
- **Our backend** (`*.supabase.co`, `api.cloudforge.app`) — only when you use a paid account, to request a one-time code and read your own subscription and quota.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data with any third party.

#### **Data Storage & Retention**

- **Local storage** — order data, PDFs, and profiles are stored in your browser using Chrome's storage API, and remain until you clear them.
- **Full local reset** — the "Clear data" button in Settings wipes everything stored on the device (cached invoices, PDFs, profiles, settings, and sign-in) after a confirmation.
- **Account data** — if you have a paid account, your email and subscription record are retained by our backend for as long as the account is active, and deleted on request or after account closure.

#### **Your Rights and Control (GDPR)**

- **Access & portability** — view and download all invoices managed by the Extension; request a copy of your account data.
- **Deletion** — clear all local data at any time via "Clear data"; request deletion of your account data by contacting us. Uninstalling the Extension removes all local data.
- **Opt-in marketing** — we send marketing emails only if you explicitly opt in, and you can withdraw consent at any time.
- **Offline use** — invoices and PDFs remain accessible offline until you clear them.

To exercise any of these rights, email contact@cloudforge.solutions.

#### **Security**

- **Local-first** — all invoice content stays on your device.
- **Minimal transmission** — only your email, authentication data, and an opaque account identifier are ever transmitted, and only for the optional paid account.
- **Minimal permissions** — the Extension requests only the permissions needed for its single purpose (see the Chrome Web Store listing).
- **Official domains only** — communication is limited to the services listed above.

#### **"Sign in with Google" (not active in this release)**

A "Sign in with Google" option may be added in a future update. It is disabled in this release, so no Google identity access occurs. If and when it ships, it will be requested only at runtime, when you choose to use it, and this policy will be updated accordingly.

#### **Children's Privacy**

The Extension is intended for business and professional use and is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

#### **Changes to This Policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted at the policy URL below and on the Chrome Web Store listing, with the "Effective Date" revised above. Please review periodically.
